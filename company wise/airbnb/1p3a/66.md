Implement a program to simulate a Connect Four game. The game is played on a grid of 6 rows and 7 columns. Players take turns placing their pieces in a chosen column, and pieces fall to the lowest empty space in the column. The goal is to connect four of one's own pieces in a row, either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally. Write a function to process a sequence of player moves and print the grid state after each move.

Input Format:

A sequence of moves, represented by integers indicating the column number.
The move sequence ends with a blank line.
Output Format:

Print the grid state after each move using 'X' and 'O' for each player’s pieces and '.' for empty spaces.
