# Intuit Staff Software Engineer Interview

## 1. Rounds

| # | Round | Duration |
|---|-------|----------|
| 1 | Phone Screen | 75 mins |
| 2 | Craft Demo | 75 mins |
| 3 | System Design | 60 mins |
| 4 | AI Accessor Interview | 30 mins |
| 5 | Hiring Manager | 30 mins |

---

## 2. Experience Snippets Per Round

### Round 1: Phone Screen (75 mins)
**Source:** [LeetCode - Intuit USA Staff Offer](https://leetcode.com/discuss/post/7368253/intuit-usa-staff-offer-by-anonymous_user-fwp7/)

- 1-2 Behavioral questions
- Standard coding problem (LeetCode medium level, straightforward)
- Follow-ups on scalability of the system, potential issues, etc.
- 20-25 mins of AI questions (if AI background):
  - AI native vs non-AI native apps discussion
  - What are LLMs
  - How do you use AI in day-to-day life
  - Dangers of AI

---

### Round 2: Craft Demo (75 mins)
**Source:** [LeetCode - Intuit USA Staff Offer](https://leetcode.com/discuss/post/7368253/intuit-usa-staff-offer-by-anonymous_user-fwp7/)

- **Intro Presentation (10 mins):** A 3-5 slide presentation of your background, hobbies, proudest project
- **Coding Exercise:**
  - They give you a repository to setup in advance (simple SpringBoot application)
  - Some endpoints defined to fetch data from a datastore
  - Given user stories to implement (add more APIs)
  - Focus: How you solve problems, what you test, how you design classes
  - Running/verifying or adding unit tests is a bonus, not mandatory
  - OK to use ChatGPT, but focus on what parts to test and how (not syntax)

---

### Round 3: System Design (60 mins)
**Source:** [LeetCode - Intuit USA Staff Offer](https://leetcode.com/discuss/post/7368253/intuit-usa-staff-offer-by-anonymous_user-fwp7/)

- Focus on scalability and high level design
- In-depth discussion on:
  - Schema design
  - Pros and cons of each proposal
  - Expanding original service to add more features
  - How DB would change
  - Query performance
  - Database optimizations for searches
  - Indexing strategies

---

### Round 4: AI Accessor Interview (30 mins)
**Source:** [LeetCode - Intuit USA Staff Offer](https://leetcode.com/discuss/post/7368253/intuit-usa-staff-offer-by-anonymous_user-fwp7/)

- Adding an AI-related follow up to the service built in Craft Demo
- Service uses an LLM API
- Write an API to support AI use case related to recommendations
- Discussion topics:
  - RAG (Retrieval Augmented Generation)
  - LLM hallucination
  - Prompt engineering

---

### Round 5: Hiring Manager (30 mins)
**Source:** [LeetCode - Intuit USA Staff Offer](https://leetcode.com/discuss/post/7368253/intuit-usa-staff-offer-by-anonymous_user-fwp7/)

- Standard behavioral interview with VP
- Questions:
  - What excites you at work
  - What are your superpowers
  - General behavioral questions

---

## Key Takeaways
- More design/architecture focus for Senior/Staff candidates instead of too much focus on coding
- Completing e2e with unit tests is not mandatory - partial implementation with good discussion can still get positive feedback
- Interviewers were a delight to talk to
- Offer came 2 days after interview
