Given two arrays rates and prices, return the index i where rates[i] / prices[i] is the maximum. The output should be the index, not the ratio itself.

Example:

rates = [1, 2, 3]

prices = [10, 2, 6]

Output: 1

Explanation: rates[1]/prices[1] = 2/2 = 1 is the maximum ratio.
