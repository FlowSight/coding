# Rubrik — HM Round Prep (SSE)
**Source**: Recruiter prep call  
**Round**: Hiring Manager

---

## Evaluation Pillars

### Company Values (5 Pillars)
1. **Velocity** — short-term vs long-term tradeoffs
2. **Relentless** — persistence, drive
3. **Integrity** — honesty, doing the right thing
4. **Excellence** — high bar for quality
5. **Transparency** — open communication

### Key Assessment Areas
- Expertise and leadership
- Culture fit
- Maturity (behavioral + technical)

---

## Expected Questions

### Projects & Complexity
- Recent project, complex problem
- Challenging project: very high complexity
- Project start to end — why needed to build the feature, design whys, approach details
- Depth of work vs breadth of work (balance)

### Decision-Making & Independence
- Independent decision you made
- How did you contribute to a common goal?

### Conflict & Failure
- Handling conflict
- Failure story — learning and how you applied it

### People & Growth
- If someone is put into PIP, how can we help them?
- Area of improvement
- Where do you see yourself in 5 years?

### Motivation
- Why change? Why Rubrik? Why not another team in Microsoft?
- Go-getter / self-motivated examples

### Technical Depth
- Might want to know how multithreading was used
- Design + code review experience
- Managing juniors / HLD ownership

---

## Format
- Intro: ~5 min
- Core questions: ~40 min
- Exit / Q&A: ~5 min

---

## What They're Looking For

| Signal | Evidence |
|--------|----------|
| Depth + complex project | AI/Agent work, perf scaling |
| Design + code review | Ownership of reviews, HLD |
| Manage junior / HLD | Mentoring, tech leadership |
| Reason for change | Clear, non-negative narrative |
| Go-getter / self-motivator | Proactive examples |

---

## Caution
- Balance depth vs breadth — don't over-index on one
